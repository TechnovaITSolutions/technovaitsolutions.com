Recall Fun - Master Your Memory

Challenge Your Brain with Fun Card Sequences!

Recall Fun is the engaging memory training game that turns mental exercise into an exciting challenge. Watch card sequences, memorise the order, then test your recall skills as you progress from simple 3-card sequences to challenging 100-card marathons.

Why Choose Recall Fun?

  • Multiple Themed Decks: Country Flags, Animals, Playing Cards, and more beautiful designs
  • Customisable Difficulty: Adjust sequence length, card speed, and complexity
  • Progress Tracking: Detailed statistics show your memory improvement over time
  • Star System: Mark your favourite decks for quick access
  • Perfect for All Ages: From daily brain training to educational fun

How to Play

  1. Select a card deck from the beautiful collection
  2. Choose your difficulty settings
  3. Watch the countdown, then memorise the card sequence
  4. Tap the cards back in the exact same order
  5. Track your progress and challenge yourself with longer sequences!

Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Tap any locked deck or visit the Settings screen to see upgrade options. The Pro upgrade is a one-time purchase that unlocks all premium features.

Can I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Yes! Your Pro purchase is linked to your Apple ID. Simply tap "Restore Purchases" in the upgrade screen on your new device.

How do I star my favourite decks?

Tap the star icon next to any deck name in the deck selection screen. Starred decks appear at the top of your list for quick access.

My statistics aren't showing - what's wrong?

Statistics begin tracking after you complete your first game. If you've played games but don't see stats, try restarting the app.

The app crashed or won't start - help!

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and restart it
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
4. If problems persist, contact us below

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2025

1. Information We Collect

Recall Fun is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

Information Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Game statistics and progress
  • App settings and preferences
  • Deck favourites and customisations
  • Purchase information (handled by Apple)

2. How We Use Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and statistics
  • Remember your app preferences
  • Provide personalised gameplay experience
  • Enable Pro features if purchased

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using Apple's standard iOS data protection. We do not transmit any personal information to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Recall Fun uses Apple's App Store and in-app purchase system. Apple's privacy policy governs any data they collect during transactions. We do not integrate any other third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

5. Children's Privacy

Recall Fun is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults. Parents can safely allow children to use the app without privacy concerns.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You delete the app
  • You reset statistics within the app
  • You restore your device

We cannot access or delete your data as it's stored locally on your device.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • View your data anytime in the app's Statistics section
  • Delete your data by resetting statistics or deleting the app
  • Export is not applicable as data remains on your device

8. International Users

Recall Fun can be used worldwide. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there are no international data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
